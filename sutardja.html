Introduction

The Pantas and Ting Sutardja Center for Entrepreneurship & Technology is the premiere institution on the UC Berkeley campus for the study and practice of “technology-centric” entrepreneurship and innovation. Since 2005, SCET has created the foundation of Berkeley’s entrepreneurship ecosystem including SkyDeck, the Fung Institute, the Engineering Leadership Professional Program, Global Venture Lab, and an extensive ecosystem of Silicon Valley and Global partners.

This year, I began working at the Sutardja Center as a technology associate and graphic designer. In my capacity as such, I’ve developed a sizable collection of materials for both online and print distribution for the Center’s programs and events.

Website Redesign
The new Sutardja Center website features responsive design and utilizes full screen elements to provide a clean aesthetic and readable content. The previously unnavigable website has also been reorganized for ease of use, especially through more intuitive layouts that have deference to established current web conventions.
Certificates
In accordance with the recent rebranding of the Center of Entrepreneurship and Technology to the Sutardja Center, I was tasked with integrating the new color schemes and general design aesthetics into all the previously existing templates for certificates and documents.
