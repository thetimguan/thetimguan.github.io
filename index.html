About Tim Guan

Hi there! I’m Tim. I'm a graphic and interaction designer specializing in UI/UX and web development. You can find my resumé here.

Right now, I’m in my third year as an undergraduate student at UC Berkeley, where I’m pursuing majors in cognitive neuroscience and molecular biology with a minor in computer science. Thematically, I study the mind at the interface of these disciplines, and how their combined knowledge informs such diverse fields as medicine, academic research, and human-centered design.

This website houses an assortment of my past works and projects.

Thanks for visiting!
