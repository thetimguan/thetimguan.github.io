About Tim Guan

Hi there! I’m Tim. I'm a product manager with a background in human-centered design. For a short summary of what I've done, you can find my resumé here.

Right now, I’m in my fourth year as an undergraduate student at UC Berkeley, where I’m pursuing majors in cognitive neuroscience and molecular biology with a minor in computer science. Thematically, I study the mind at the interface of these disciplines, and how their combined knowledge informs such diverse fields as medicine, neuroscience, and human-centered design.

This website houses an assortment of my past works and projects.

Thanks for visiting!
